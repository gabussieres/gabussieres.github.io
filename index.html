Biography

Gabriel Bussieres is an undergraduate student in his third year at the University of Toronto, pursuing a Specialist degree in Computer Science with a focus in Artificial Intelligence. He grew up in Woodstock, Ontario and was educated in a French school system. At Ecole Secondaire Notre Dame (secondary school), he participated in the High Skills Major in Non-profit services, as well as the student government where he played the role of Minister of Finance and Secretary. His admiration for mathematics and programming led him to study Computer Science.

Gabriel exhibited on many occasions, the ability to work collaboratively with other students during class projects, school activities and community events. For example, he played a significant role in the organization of two city-wide junior high dances that were held at ESND. As well, he assumed a leadership role during classroom collaborative assignments, often as the problem-solver of the group.
