Biography

I am a third year undergraduate student at the University of Toronto, pursuing a Specialist in Computer Science and a Minor in Psychology. My goal is to complete a focus in Artificial Intelligence, which I am starting to explore in my Winter 2016 courses. I grew up in Woodstock, Ontario and was educated in a French school system. At Ecole Secondaire Notre Dame (secondary school), I participated in the High Skills Major in Non-profit services, as well as the student government where I took the role of Minister of Finance and Secretary. My admiration for mathematics and programming led to my studies in Computer Science.

Gabriel exhibited on many occasions, the ability to work collaboratively with other students during class projects, school activities and community events. For example, he played a significant role in the organization of two city-wide junior high dances that were held at ESND. As well, he assumed a leadership role during classroom collaborative assignments, often as the problem-solver of the group.
